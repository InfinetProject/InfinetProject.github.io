Decentralising The Internet One Step At A Time

Our Mission

Currently almost everyone is paying a small fortune for a slow, laggy internet connection. The Infinet project was designed to make a free, open internet for everyone without need for an ISP or centeral corperation. Founded by JKinc and Zeon, The Infinet Project hopes to free the internet and put it in the hands of the users and not in the hands of big tech companies!
